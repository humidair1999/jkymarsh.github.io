personal statement

I'm a passionate, motivated, neurotic, perfectionistic Javascript and front-end developer who's always looking for my next challenge.

Having been writing JS for the better part of a decade, I favor OO principles and a wide variety of JS-based libraries, toolkits, and frameworks: Backbone (and Backbone.Marionette), Angular, jQuery, jQueryUI, a wide variety of third-party JS and jQuery plugins and extensions, and unit testing libs such as Jasmine, Sinon, and Mocha are all within my repertoire.

Despite having considerable experience with Node.js, PHP, and Ruby (RoR), the majority of my professional experience is with the Microsoft stack (ASP.NET/IIS/Visual Studio/TFS), working on ASP.NET MVC-based projects, as well as WebAPI-based projects and SharePoint sites. I've even written a brief book on front-end development within SharePoint!
