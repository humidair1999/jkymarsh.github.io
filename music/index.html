Music

Past & current projects

In addition to graduating from Northern Illinois University with a BA in Music, I've played in bands since I was a teenager back in the suburbs of central Maine. Though I'm primarily a drummer, I've been a frontman for a handful of hardcore and metal bands since those early days as well.

My current project is the shoegaze/post-rock/punk band Bad Visuals, based out of Oakland. Our releases can be streamed below.
