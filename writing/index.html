Writing

Past & current projects

I've been writing various forms of prose and poetry for over two decades. Influenced by Pablo Neruda, Anne Sexton, E. E. Cummings, Elinor Wiley, John Keats, as well as various modern contemporary poets, I enjoy crafting nostalgic pieces that sting of the pains of failed romance and better days gone by.

I'm presently in the midst of finishing a 60-piece book of poetry consisting of work written over a span of about three years. A release date has yet to be set, but a few samples can be read below in the meantime.
